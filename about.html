Our Philosophy

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

At Mudra Junction, our mission is to simplify financial access by delivering tailored loan solutions with integrity, efficiency, and transparency. We strive to empower individuals and businesses through responsible financing, foster long-term relationships built on trust, leverage technology for hassle-free services, and contribute to financial inclusion by extending support to underserved segments of society.

Our Vision

To become a leading and trusted financial institution that empowers individuals and businesses to achieve their goals through innovative, transparent, and customer-centric financial solutions. We envision a future where access to finance is seamless, responsible, and inclusive — helping every customer turn aspiration into achievements.

Growth Journey

Our Journey Timeline

From inception to becoming a trusted financial partner

May 2021 – April 2023

Foundational Years

Started as a Corporate DSA for leading Fintech firms and NBFCs. Established presence across Uttar Pradesh with Personal Loans, Business Loans, and Affordable Housing Loans.

₹9 Crore Business Corporate DSA UP Presence
May 2023 – March 2024

Expansion & Growth

Transitioned to Lending Service Provider (LSP) partnership with Arthmate. Expanded to 8 new branches and achieved ₹100 Crore AUM in Unsecured Business Loans.

₹100 Cr AUM 8 New Branches LSP Model
April 2024 – Present

Diversification & Strategic Growth

Expanded partnerships as Business Correspondent with Pinkcity, Samunnati, and DMI Housing Finance. Grew to 11 branches and acquired NBFC - Raghubar Automobile & General Finance Company Pvt. Ltd.

11 Branches NBFC Acquisition Multi-Partner
2025 & Beyond

Future Forward

Building an AI-powered NBFC with focus on Secured Loans, Balance Transfer, and Unsecured Business Loans. Integrating advanced analytics for better decision-making and customer experience.

Governance

Corporate Governance Principles

We operate with full transparency in all our operations, ensuring that our financials, decisions, and actions are clearly communicated to stakeholders. Our governance structure ensures that we are accountable for our performance, both socially and financially.

Ethical Conduct

We uphold ethical conduct in every aspect of our business, from financial reporting to customer interactions. Our management team, directors, and employees adhere to a strict code of conduct that promotes integrity, fairness, and professionalism.

Compliance

We comply with all applicable laws and regulations set by regulatory authorities like the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), SEBI, MFIN, and other relevant bodies. This compliance ensures transparent and responsible financial services.

Risk Management

We maintain a robust risk management framework that identifies, assesses, and mitigates risks across operations. We continuously monitor financial, operational, and strategic risks to ensure sustainable growth.
