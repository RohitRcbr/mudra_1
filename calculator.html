What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed payment amount made by a borrower to a lender at a specified date each calendar month.

Factors Affecting EMI

  • Loan Amount
  • Interest Rate
  • Loan Tenure
  • Type of Interest (Fixed/Floating)

Tips for Better Planning

  • Keep EMI under 40% of income
  • Consider prepayment options
  • Compare loan offers
  • Check for hidden charges
